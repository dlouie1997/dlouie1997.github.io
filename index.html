Digital Culture: Graphic Information Technology graduate at Arizona State University

As someone who has toyed around on websites since middle school, it has always been fun to see the different types of websites I could create. Giving each and everyone their own personality, feeling, and style was something I enjoyed, even if was on website builders that did most of the work for you. Now a days, I'm learning how to hard code websites, gaining more control over how the sites looks, and being able to make it look exactly as I want it to.

In addition to using HTML and CSS (with basic and in training knowledge of JavaScript), I am also familiar with many Adobe products. Some of my strengths are video editing in Premiere Pro, manipulating photos in Photoshop, using VScode and basical terminal commands, and building emails from a mockup, like in Adobe XD. I am familiar with using Monday, Slack, Teams and other similar applications. I have knowledge in Illustrator, components (what you see is what you get), and am actively learning Figma.

I also have a passion for video editing and would love to try and learn and grow in that industry where possible.
